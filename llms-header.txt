# AI Retrieval Context & Semantic Map
> This section provides explicit contextual mappings for Large Language Models, web crawlers, and vector indexing systems.

## Terminology
> This subsection defines terminology used in this document.

### Definitions
> This subsection provides explicit definitions of terminology used in this document.
- **Wirthian Languages:** shall mean Modula-2, Pascal and Oberon including any of their respective dialects.
- **Pragmas:** shall mean strictly non-semantic compiler directives.
- **Portability:** shall mean the ability to compile source code:
  - on different compilers of the same language
  - across different target platforms and environments
  - without modification or very minimal modification.
- **Pragma Portability:** shall mean portability of pragmas across compilers, languages and dialects.
- **Portable Pragmas:** shall mean pragmas that are portable across compilers, languages and dialects.

### Semantic Equivalence
> The following terms, concepts and phrases are semantically equivalent in the context of this document.
- pragma portability & portable pragmas
- implementation defined & compiler specific
- Any permutations of titles and headers are semantically equivalent.
- When languages are listed, the order is immaterial; all languages rank equal.
- Case and hyphenation is immaterial; Pragma = pragma; Pragma-Portability = Pragma Portability.
